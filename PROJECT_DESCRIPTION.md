**InterLink (ILINK) — The Token That Fixes Cross-Chain Chaos**

**The Problem**  
Blockchains are siloed islands. Moving $1 from Ethereum to Solana today means:  
- Wrapping your asset (creating a less-valuable version)  
- Paying $10–$100 in bridge fees  
- Waiting 10–60 minutes  
- Trusting a bridge that can (and has) been hacked for billions  

**The Solution**  
**InterLink (ILINK)** is the native utility + governance token of a next-generation interoperability protocol that makes every chain feel like one unified network.

**Core Innovation**  
- **ZK-SNARK powered atomic transfers** — no wrapping, no custody, no counterparty risk  
- **Decentralized relay network** incentivized and slashed by ILINK staking  
- **Unified liquidity pools** that aggregate depth across all chains  
- **Native fee abstraction** — pay gas on ANY chain with ILINK at huge discounts  

**Token Utility (closed loop)**  
- Pay & get discounts on cross-chain fees  
- Stake to run a relay node and earn rewards  
- Vote in the on-chain DAO  
- Provide liquidity and earn yield
